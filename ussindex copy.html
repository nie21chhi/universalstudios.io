    How to get here?

    Address: Resorts World Sentosa, 8 Sentosa Gateway, Sentosa Island, Singapore 098269

    1. On Sentosa Gateway, keep to left lane and drive down the slope leading to Resorts World Sentosa Car Park. 2. Filter right and drive into tunnel for "Cars/Taxis". 3. Follow overhead signage to "B1 East" and park vehicle. 4. Locate nearest Car Park Map Directory and follow directions to Universal Studios Singapore on level 1.

    Carpark charges: Monday - Thursday (excludes Public Holidays) 12am to 11:59pm: $6 per entry Friday/Saturday/Sunday/Public Holiday 12am to 11.59pm: $9 per entry
